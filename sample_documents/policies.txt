Company Policies and Procedures

RETURN POLICY

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee
We stand behind our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Return Window
- Standard products: 30 days from purchase date
- Electronic items: 15 days from purchase date
- Custom/personalized items: Non-returnable unless defective
- Digital products: Non-returnable unless technically defective

Return Conditions
Items must be:
- In original condition and packaging
- Accompanied by original receipt or proof of purchase
- Free from damage not related to manufacturing defects
- Complete with all accessories and documentation

Return Process
1. Contact customer service to initiate return
2. Receive return authorization number (RMA)
3. Package item securely with RMA number
4. Ship using provided prepaid label (for defective items)
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

Refund Methods
- Original payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- Store credit (at customer's request)
- Exchange for different item (subject to price difference)

Exceptions
- Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
- Products modified or altered by customer
- Items without proof of purchase
- Returned after applicable return window

SHIPPING POLICY

Domestic Shipping
- Free standard shipping on orders $50+
- Standard shipping (3-5 business days): $5.99
- Express shipping (1-2 business days): $9.99
- Overnight shipping (next business day): $19.99

International Shipping
- Available to most countries
- Shipping costs calculated at checkout
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Customer responsible for duties and taxes

Processing Time
- Standard orders: 1-2 business days
- Custom orders: 3-5 business days
- Back-ordered items: 7-10 business days
- Orders placed after 2PM EST ship next business day

Shipping Restrictions
Some items cannot be shipped to:
- P.O. Boxes (for certain products)
- Military APO/FPO addresses (restrictions apply)
- International destinations (country-specific restrictions)
